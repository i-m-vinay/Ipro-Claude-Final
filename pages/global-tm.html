Madrid Protocol

One Application.
100+ Countries.

The Madrid Protocol is an international treaty administered by WIPO that lets you file a single trademark application protecting your brand across all designated member countries — managed from your home trademark office.

Key Advantages

Single application for 100+ countries
Centralised management & renewals
Cost-effective vs direct country filing
Streamlined international prosecution
Extend to more countries later with ease
